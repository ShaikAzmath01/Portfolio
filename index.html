About Me

  • Hey there! I'm Shaik Azmath, a passionate explorer in the world of Organic Chemistry, always on the lookout for exciting opportunities in Regulatory Affairs, R&D, and QA within the pharmaceutical domain. My journey has been about diving deep into synthesis methods, sustainability practices, and ensuring that regulations are more than just guidelines—they're ingrained in the way we innovate.

  • Education has been a cornerstone for me. I've delved into the intricacies of Organic Chemistry at A.V. College, Hyderabad, currently awaiting the culmination of my M.Sc. program. It's been eye-opening, reinforcing the importance of maintaining impeccable quality and safety standards within the industry.

  • When I'm not delving into the science behind pharmaceuticals, you might find me exploring languages—I'm fluent in English, Hindi, Telugu, Urdu, with a sprinkle of familiarity in Arabic.

  • For me, this isn't just about qualifications; it's about the curiosity to dive deeper, the drive to innovate responsibly, and the dedication to uphold quality standards in everything I do.
